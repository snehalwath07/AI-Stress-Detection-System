## Step 1: Open the Website

Open the AI Stress Detection System using the deployed Vercel link.

You will be redirected to the homepage of the application.

---

## Step 2: Explore the Homepage

The homepage contains:

* Navigation Bar
* Project Introduction
* Get Started Button
* Features Section
* About the System

Read the information to understand the purpose of the AI Stress Detection System.

---

## Step 3: Open the Assessment Page

Click on the **Assessment** option from the sidebar.

You will be redirected to the Stress Assessment page where you can enter your daily lifestyle information.

---

## Step 4: Enter Assessment Details

Fill in all the required fields:

* Sleep Hours
* Work Hours
* Screen Time
* Water Intake
* Exercise Hours
* Mood Score
* Social Interaction Score

Verify that all values are entered correctly.

---

## Step 5: Analyze Stress

Click the **Analyze Stress** button.

The system will:

* Collect user inputs
* Send data to the backend
* Run the Machine Learning model
* Predict the stress level
* Generate personalized recommendations
* Save the assessment history

The analysis takes only a few seconds.

---

## Step 6: View Prediction Result

After processing, the system displays:

* Stress Level (Low / Moderate / High)
* Personalized Health Recommendations
* Latest Assessment Result

Users can review the prediction instantly.

---

## Step 7: Open Dashboard

Navigate to the **Dashboard** page.

The dashboard displays:

* Current Stress Level
* Risk Level
* Stress Trend Graph
* AI Insights
* Recent Activity
* Wellness Statistics

---

## Step 8: View Predictions

Open the **Predictions** page.

The page displays:

* Previous Assessments
* Stress History
* Prediction Results
* Stress Trends

This helps users monitor their progress over time.

---

## Step 9: AI Copilot

Open the **AI Copilot** page.

Users can:

* Ask stress-related questions
* Receive wellness guidance
* Get healthy lifestyle suggestions
* Learn stress management techniques

---

## Step 10: Reports

Open the **Reports** page.

Users can:

* View assessment reports
* Review previous results
* Download stress reports (if enabled)

---

## Step 11: Stress Relief Hub

Navigate to the **Stress Relief Hub**.

The page provides:

* Stress management tips
* Healthy lifestyle advice

---

## Step 12: Settings

Open the **Settings** page.

Users can:

* Manage application preferences
* View account settings
* Configure system options

---

# How It Works

### Step 1

User enters daily lifestyle information.

### Step 2

The frontend sends the data to the Flask backend.

### Step 3

The Machine Learning model analyzes the information.

### Step 4

The model predicts the user's stress level.

### Step 5

The system generates personalized recommendations.

### Step 6

The assessment is stored and displayed on the dashboard.
